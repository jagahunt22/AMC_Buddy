local_airport

Airport

Ministry of Civil Aviation, New Delhi, India with an opinion of necessity towards height restriction for safe guarding of aircraft operations at Civil and Defence Airports grant ‘No Objection Certificate’ (NOC) through Airports Authority of India Act, 1994. Designated Officer from Airports Authority of India is authorised for process and issue of NOC which indicates the permissible height of construction for various structures.

issued on 26/7/2018

whatshot

Fire

According to Andhra Pradesh Fire Service Act, 1999, Residential Buildings of height more than 18 mts ( high rise buildings ), Commercial buildings of height 15 mts and above and buildings of public congregation like Educational Buildings, Cinema Theatres, Function Halls and other Assembly Buildings on plot area of 500 sqmt and above or of height above 6 mt are required to obtain prior clearance from Andhra Pradesh State Disaster Response & Fire Services Department from fire safety point of view.

issued on 26/7/2018
