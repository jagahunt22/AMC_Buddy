Environmental Clearance

SEIAA – Andhra Pradesh
Ministry of Environment & Forests, Government of India notifies in September 2006, under the Environment (Protection) Act, 1986, imposing certain restrictions and prohibitions on new projects or activities, based on their potential environment impacts, prior Environmental Clearance has been accorded in accordance with the objectives of National Environmental Policy by the Central Government State Level Environment Impact Assessment Authority (SEIAA).


Guidelines for seeking Environmental clearance for Construction Projects
Environmental clearance is necessary for a few categories of construction projects and area development projects under the new EIA Notification, 2006 as amended from time to time as per the following schedule :-


Building / Construction projects / Area Development projects and Townships
Building and Construction projects ≥ 20000 sq.mtrs and
< 1,50,000 sq.mtrs of built-up area
Townships and Area Development projects Covering an area ≥ 50 ha and or built up area ≥ 1,50,000 sq.mtrs++

Projects falling under Category ‘A’ in the Schedule to the Notification require Environmental Clearance from the Government of India, Ministry of Environment & Forests, New Delhi and Category ‘B’ Projects Notified in the Schedule require Environmental Clearance from State level Environment Impact Assessment Authority, A.P. Hyderabad.


Apart from Environment Clearance(EC), Consent for Establishment (CFE) & Consent for Operation (CFO) are required based on the category of the projects.

AP Building Rules, AP GO Ms. No.119 and State SEIAA would brief EC, CFE & CFO

FAQ FOR SEEKING NOC FOR ENVIRONMENTAL CLEARANCE

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) provides easy answers to the applicant queries, seeking Fire clearance.

Q.1.Can I submit my land allotment application online? keyboard_arrow_down

Ans. Yes, you can submit land allotment application online by logging into the site and click on Online Application link.

Q.2. How can I find out if the land I am looking for is available? keyboard_arrow_down

Ans. Yes, After login to the site, you can click on Plots Sold and Available link and search for the plot you are looking for to know its status

Q.3. I have changed my password recently but don’t remember it. What should I do next? keyboard_arrow_down

Ans. In login screen, click on Forgot Password? link and enter the email address you have registered with APIIC, you will receive a password in an email.

Q.4. I have forgotten my username, what should I do next? keyboard_arrow_down

Ans. Please contact APIIC administrator at admin@apiic.in.

Q.5. 5. I want to download few documents from “Downloads” section of APIIC site but they are big in size, Can I get a CD? keyboard_arrow_down

Ans. You will have to submit a written request to APIIC. Please contact APIIC administrator at admin@apiic.in
